Welcome.

Research in the Tellman lab seeks to understand and address the consequences of global environmental change. We use data driven approaches to understand human environment change, and assess the potential for new technologies to help reduce risk for marginalized populations. Most of our research involves satellite imagery, news media, and other data streams to quantify change.
